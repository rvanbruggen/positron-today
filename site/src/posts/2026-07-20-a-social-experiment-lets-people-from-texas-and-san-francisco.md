Imagine answering a payphone and connecting with someone over 1500 miles away! That's exactly what Matter Neuroscience is doing with their innovative social experiment. By placing payphone-like devices in Texas and San Francisco, they aim to foster friendly conversations between people from vastly different backgrounds. It's all about breaking down barriers and reminding us that, at the end of the day, we're all just humans looking for connection.
