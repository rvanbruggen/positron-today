Isn't it fascinating how love can literally change our brains? Scientists have discovered that when we fall in love, our brains rewire themselves, placing our partners in the same category as basic survival needs. This intense connection can make heartbreak feel like physical pain, as our brains struggle to adjust when a relationship ends. It's amazing to think that love is not just an emotion, but a powerful drive that shapes who we are.
