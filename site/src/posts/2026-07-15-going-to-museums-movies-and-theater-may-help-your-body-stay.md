So, it turns out that enjoying cultural activities like visiting museums and theaters might actually help keep our bodies feeling younger! A new study shows that people who engage in these activities regularly have a lower physiological age compared to those who don’t. This research highlights the importance of staying socially active for healthy aging, which is just fascinating! It seems that the joy of culture could be a key ingredient in the recipe for a longer, healthier life.
