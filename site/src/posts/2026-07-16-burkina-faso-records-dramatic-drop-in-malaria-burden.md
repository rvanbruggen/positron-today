Wow, what fantastic news from Burkina Faso! Between 2024 and 2025, malaria cases dropped by 32 percent, and deaths fell by an impressive 44 percent, especially among children under five. This remarkable progress is thanks to a combination of strategies, including the distribution of insecticide-treated mosquito nets and the introduction of malaria vaccines. With a goal to eliminate malaria by 2030, this is a huge step in the right direction!
