In Ulaanbaatar, the talented Batzorig Vaanchig is bringing Mongolian throat singing to the world stage, and it's so exciting! He's using both traditional methods and modern digital platforms to share this unique art form, which UNESCO recognizes as part of our cultural heritage. Vaanchig's performances, often accompanied by the horsehead fiddle, are captivating audiences far and wide, inspiring a new generation of singers. It's amazing to see how this once endangered practice is experiencing a revival, thanks to passionate artists like him.
