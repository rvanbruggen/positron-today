So, a fascinating discovery in Spain has unearthed eight grass-woven sandals that date back over 2,000 years! These sandals, found in an ancient mining settlement, reveal much about life during the Iron Age just before the Romans arrived. They show signs of heavy use, indicating they were the everyday footwear of laborers rather than luxury items. It's incredible how well-preserved these sandals are, giving us a rare glimpse into the past and the materials people used.
