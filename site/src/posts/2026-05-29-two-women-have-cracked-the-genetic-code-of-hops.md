So, here's the exciting news! A team of international researchers, led by Danish scientists, has successfully mapped the genetic code of hops, which is a crucial ingredient in beer. This breakthrough could pave the way for non-alcoholic beers that taste just like a classic pilsner. Imagine enjoying a refreshing beer without the alcohol but with all the flavor! It's a fascinating development that could also lead to climate-resistant hop plants.
