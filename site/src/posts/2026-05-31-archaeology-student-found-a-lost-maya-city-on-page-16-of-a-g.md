Isn't it amazing how a simple Google search can lead to incredible discoveries? Luke Auld-Thomas, a PhD student, stumbled upon a lost Mayan city called Valeriana while scrolling through page 16 of search results. This ancient metropolis, hidden under forest cover, housed tens of thousands of people and is challenging our understanding of civilization in tropical regions. With LiDAR technology, researchers are uncovering more secrets of the past, and who knows what else is waiting to be found online!
