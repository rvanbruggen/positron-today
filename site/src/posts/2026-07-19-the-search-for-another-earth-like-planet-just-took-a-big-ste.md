Astronomers have made an exciting discovery by confirming a rocky planet with an atmosphere in the habitable zone, just 48 light-years away! This exoplanet, LHS 1140 b, might be the closest thing to Earth we’ve found so far. Researchers detected helium signatures, indicating it retains an atmosphere that has existed for at least 3 billion years. While it’s not proof of life, this finding opens up new possibilities for understanding habitable worlds beyond our solar system.
