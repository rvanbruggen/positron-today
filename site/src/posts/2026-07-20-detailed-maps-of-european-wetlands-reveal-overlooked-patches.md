So, researchers have used satellite images and machine-learning algorithms to create high-resolution maps of European wetlands. This is fantastic news for conservation efforts, as these wetlands are crucial for carbon storage. They may only cover a small percentage of the Earth's surface, but they play a big role in our ecosystem. These maps will serve as a vital tool for restoration projects, helping to protect these precious environments.
