Ecstatic dance is taking the UK by storm, blending spirituality, fun, and a great workout into one joyful experience. Picture this: a Sunday morning filled with music, movement, and no alcohol – just pure expression! Participants of all ages gather to shake off the week's stresses, and many find it liberating and uplifting. It's not just a dance; it's a community where everyone can connect without the pressure of conversation.
