So, did you know that children in Finland don’t start formal schooling until they’re seven? It’s fascinating how they enjoy shorter school days, regular play breaks, and even free nutritious meals! Instead of cramming for exams, Finnish schools focus on critical thinking and personalized support, which leads to happier learners. This approach not only ranks them among the best education systems but also encourages a love for learning—what a refreshing perspective!
