Australia is leading a remarkable revolution in home renewable energy and battery use, which is already driving down electricity prices. With nearly 60% of new household battery capacity installed this year, the country is proving that the right policies can make a significant impact. As solar and wind energy take over, batteries are stepping in to store energy for when it's needed most. This shift not only counters the unpredictability of renewables but also positions Australia as a global leader in sustainable energy solutions.
