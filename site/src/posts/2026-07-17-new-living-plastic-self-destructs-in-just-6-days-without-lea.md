So, researchers have developed a fascinating new type of plastic that can break itself down in just six days! This 'living plastic' contains dormant microbes that, when activated, produce enzymes to decompose the material without leaving behind pesky microplastics. It's a brilliant solution to the long-lasting problem of traditional plastics, especially for short-lived items like packaging. Imagine a world where our plastic waste could simply vanish when it's no longer needed!
