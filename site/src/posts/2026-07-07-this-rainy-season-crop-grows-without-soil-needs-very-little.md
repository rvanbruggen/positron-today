As the monsoon rains arrive in India, farmers are discovering the joys of mushroom farming. These fungi thrive in controlled environments, making them perfect for small-scale growers and home enthusiasts. With the right humidity and temperature, mushrooms like oyster and paddy straw flourish, turning agricultural waste into nutritious food. It's an exciting opportunity for anyone looking to dive into sustainable farming during the rainy season!
