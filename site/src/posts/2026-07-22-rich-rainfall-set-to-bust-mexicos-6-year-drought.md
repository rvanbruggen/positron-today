So, it looks like Mexico is on track to shake off a long-lasting drought thanks to some rich rainfall this season! The El Niño effect has brought wetter conditions from the north to the south, and researchers are reporting a remarkable 94% recovery from drought conditions. Just a tiny area in Chihuahua is still feeling the effects, but overall, only 2.4% of the country is experiencing drought. This is fantastic news for the people and the environment alike!
