So, there's this fascinating pet gecko that might just be the key to understanding cancer better! Researchers found that this colorful little guy, known as the lemon frost morph, develops tumors naturally, which could provide insights into why some species are more cancer-prone than others. The study highlights how genetic changes in these geckos are linked to human cancers, making them a unique model for research. It's exciting to think that studying these reptiles could inspire new ways to prevent and treat cancer in humans!
