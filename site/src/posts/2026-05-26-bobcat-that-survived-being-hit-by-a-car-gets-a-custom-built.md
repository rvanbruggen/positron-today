A Pennsylvania bobcat that survived a terrible car accident is getting a new lease on life thanks to a custom-built kennel. After two months of recovery at Raven Ridge Wildlife Center, she needed a safe space to exercise and regain her strength. Miraculously, a local kennel builder stepped in to create the enclosure in just two weeks. To top it off, a generous donation in memory of a supporter covered the costs, allowing the bobcat to thrive in her new home.
