It's fascinating to see how evidence-based medicine has transformed healthcare over the decades! Just think about it - fifty years ago, doctors believed babies should sleep on their stomachs, but research proved that sleeping on their backs significantly reduces the risk of SIDS. Helen Pearson's new book dives into these incredible stories of change, showcasing how solid evidence has corrected long-held misconceptions. It's a real eye-opener, revealing the mavericks who faced resistance while championing these vital practices.
