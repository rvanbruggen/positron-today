In a remarkable turn of events, California has reintroduced beavers after 70 years, inspired by ancient wisdom from Native American art. This initiative, driven by the Tule River Tribe and the Maidu Summit Consortium, aims to tackle modern water conservation challenges. The first seven beavers were released in October 2023, and their impact has been impressive, creating wetlands that enhance water coverage significantly. It's a beautiful example of how looking to the past can inform sustainable practices for the future.
