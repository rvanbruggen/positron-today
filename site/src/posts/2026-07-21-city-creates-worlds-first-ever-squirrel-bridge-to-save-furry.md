Every year, millions of squirrels meet unfortunate fates on the roads, but Longview, Washington, decided to take action! Architect Amos Peters led the charge in creating the Nutty Narrows Squirrel Pedestrian Bridge, a clever solution to help these little critters cross safely. This bridge not only became a beloved tourist attraction but also inspired more bridges, including a recent one in nearby Duvall. It's heartwarming to see communities come together to protect wildlife and ensure that these adorable squirrels can thrive.
