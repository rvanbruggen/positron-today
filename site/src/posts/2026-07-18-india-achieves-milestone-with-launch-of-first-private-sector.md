India has just made a giant leap in the space race by successfully launching its first private-sector orbital rocket, the Vikram-1. This impressive 22-meter rocket took off from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre and placed payloads into low-Earth orbit, making India the third country to achieve this through private enterprise. Prime Minister Modi expressed his excitement, stating this achievement will inspire young innovators to dream big. With plans for more test flights ahead, the future of India's space ambitions looks incredibly bright!
