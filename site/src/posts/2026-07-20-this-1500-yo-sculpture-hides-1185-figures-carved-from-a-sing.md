So, hidden in Eran, Madhya Pradesh, is this incredible 1,500-year-old sculpture from the Gupta era. It's a massive Varaha figure, standing 11 feet tall, and it’s carved from a single block of sandstone! What’s really fascinating is that it features 1,185 miniature figures, including sages and saints, all meticulously arranged. Plus, there's an ancient Sanskrit inscription on its neck that tells a remarkable story from a turbulent time in history.
