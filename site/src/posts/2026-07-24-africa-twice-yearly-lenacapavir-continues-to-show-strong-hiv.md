Twice-yearly injectable lenacapavir is making waves in HIV prevention, showing almost no new infections in recent trials. The PURPOSE-1 and PURPOSE-2 studies highlight its effectiveness and safety, raising hopes for a transformative impact on global HIV prevention. With over 95% participant acceptance in the open-label extension, adherence remains impressively high. This breakthrough could change the landscape of HIV prevention, especially for women.
