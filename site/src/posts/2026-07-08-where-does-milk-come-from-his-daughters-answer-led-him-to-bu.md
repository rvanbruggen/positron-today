Most kids know what's on their plate but not where it comes from, which is what sparked Kiruba Shankar's journey. After a humorous yet concerning moment with his daughter, he transformed his family's abandoned land into Vaksana Farms in Tamil Nadu. This isn't just a day trip; families get to live farm life for 24 hours, diving into hands-on activities that teach them about agriculture. Children learn the importance of every part of a farm, getting their hands dirty and truly connecting with the soil.
