How amazing is this? The UK's first rooftop intensive care ward has opened at King's College Hospital, allowing patients like Hollie to experience the outdoors while receiving critical care. Hollie, who spent two months in intensive care, was overwhelmed with joy when she felt the sunshine for the first time. This innovative space not only supports patient recovery but also offers a much-needed break for the staff. It's a beautiful step towards enhancing the healing environment in hospitals.
