In the heart of East London, a vibrant collection of mosaics is transforming lives through art. The Hackney Mosaic Project, founded by Tessa Hunkin, brings together individuals dealing with PTSD and other mental health challenges to create stunning public artworks. This therapeutic process not only beautifies the community but also fosters connection and healing among participants. Each mosaic is a testament to resilience, showcasing the power of creativity in overcoming personal struggles.
