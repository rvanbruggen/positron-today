When heavy rains cause chaos in cities like Mumbai, the East Kolkata Wetlands stand out as a natural solution. Covering 12,500 hectares, these wetlands absorb excess rainwater and treat sewage, proving that nature can be our best ally against flooding. They support local livelihoods through sustainable practices like fish farming and agriculture, creating a circular economy. As urban flooding becomes more frequent, the wetlands remind us of the importance of preserving natural ecosystems for climate resilience.
