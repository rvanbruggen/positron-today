What an exciting day for mathematics! The 2026 Fields Medals were awarded to four brilliant young mathematicians at the International Congress of Mathematicians in Philadelphia. Among them, Hong Wang made history as only the third woman to receive this prestigious honor, showcasing her groundbreaking work on the Kakeya conjecture. It's inspiring to see such talent recognized, especially with the awards celebrating achievements under 40 years old, which really highlights the future of math. This event not only honors individual accomplishments but also brings the global math community together in a vibrant celebration.
