Imagine if every place took cleanliness seriously! In Lachen, Sikkim, they check vehicles for single-use plastic bottles before heading to Gurudongmar Lake, which is such a fantastic way to protect this stunning glacial lake from pollution. This initiative builds on Sikkim's groundbreaking plastic bag ban from 1998. Through community involvement and eco-friendly options like bamboo bottles, Lachen is proving that caring for nature can be a lifestyle, not just a rule.
