In the UAE, a massive clean energy project is set to power half a million homes at night using solar energy and the largest battery scheme in the world. Meanwhile, researchers in Colorado are creating tiny batteries that could track young salmon and eel species, showcasing the diversity of energy storage solutions. Unlike traditional lithium-ion batteries, these alternatives can be recycled and used indefinitely, which is fantastic for sustainability. Innovations like the Carrington 'liquid air' cryobattery and molten salt storage in Nevada are leading the charge in harnessing renewable energy without the environmental downsides.
