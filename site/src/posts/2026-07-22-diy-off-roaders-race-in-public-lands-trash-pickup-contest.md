Every summer, a wild group of car enthusiasts gathers in Oregon for the Gambler 500, where they show off their quirky DIY vehicles and compete to clean up public lands. This year, they managed to remove a staggering 250,000 pounds of trash, bringing their total to an impressive 5 million pounds since the event began! Participants drive cars bought for $500 or less, and some even come with outrageous modifications like flames and dragon decorations. It's all about having fun while making a positive impact on the environment, which is just fantastic!
