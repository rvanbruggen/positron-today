So, France has made a wonderful gesture by returning 23 Syrian antique artefacts that were held since the civil war began in 2011. This collection, which showcases history from prehistory to the Abbasid era, is now back where it belongs at the National Museum in Damascus. It’s fantastic to see cultural heritage being restored, especially after such a tumultuous period. This move follows a visit from French President Emmanuel Macron, highlighting the importance of international cooperation in preserving history.
