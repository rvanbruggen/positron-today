Imagine this: you can swap cigarette butts for delicious Dutch pancakes! That's exactly what the WasteBar is doing at festivals in the Netherlands to tackle litter. With catchy slogans and a fun vibe, they're turning a common waste problem into a creative solution. It's all about sparking a change in mentality around littering, and so far, they've collected over 500,000 butts. How cool is that?
