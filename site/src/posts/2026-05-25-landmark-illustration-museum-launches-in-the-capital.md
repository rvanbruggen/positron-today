I'm really excited about the opening of the world's largest museum dedicated to illustration, set to launch in Clerkenwell, London, this June! British illustrator Quentin Blake has poured over 20 years into making this dream a reality, and it features three galleries celebrating the art of illustration. The opening exhibition will showcase the work of Murugiah, a talented British-Sri Lankan artist exploring identity and mental health. Plus, the centre will include a free public library and learning studios, making it a vibrant hub for creativity!
