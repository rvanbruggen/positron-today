Poland has a staggering 38,000 square kilometers of post-industrial land that is ripe for revitalization. Traditionally seen as burdensome, these areas are now being transformed into local energy production sites, which is super exciting! For instance, the municipality of Przykona has launched a massive solar farm on reclaimed land from a coal mine. This shift not only benefits the environment but also empowers local communities by providing them with renewable energy tailored to their needs.
