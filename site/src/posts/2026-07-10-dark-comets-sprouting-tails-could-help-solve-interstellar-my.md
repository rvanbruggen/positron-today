Astronomers have been puzzled by the strange behavior of ‘Oumuamua since its flyby in 2017. Instead of slowing down, it sped up, leading to wild theories about alien spacecraft. Now, researchers have observed dark comets sprouting tails, providing evidence that these objects could explain such anomalies. This discovery not only sheds light on ‘Oumuamua but also helps clarify the relationship between comets and asteroids in our solar system.
