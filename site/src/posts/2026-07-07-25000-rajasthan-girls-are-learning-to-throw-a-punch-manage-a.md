It's amazing to see how the Rani Laxmi Bai Kendra is empowering young women in Rajasthan! This program combines self-defense, legal literacy, and financial skills, giving students the confidence to navigate life independently. Chetna Jogi, a BSc student, shares how this initiative has transformed her perspective and opened up a world of possibilities. With over 25,000 girls participating, it's clear that this program is making a significant impact on their futures.
