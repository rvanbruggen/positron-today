Imagine sipping chestnut kir while watching beavers swim in the crystal-clear Tarn River! This new five-day hiking route through the Gorges du Tarn offers stunning views and a chance to connect with nature. With over 30 varieties of orchids and a vibrant wildlife scene, it’s a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Plus, you’ll encounter charming medieval towns and delicious local cuisine along the way.
