So, what if our cities could adapt like nature? Mina Hasman, an award-winning architect, shares her inspiring vision of urban environments that function like forests and buildings that breathe like trees. She emphasizes the urgent need for cities to embrace climate-ready designs that promote thriving communities and harmonious relationships with nature. This approach could revolutionize how we think about urban living in a warming world.
