Until now, only 18 such circumbinary planets were known, so this discovery is a big leap forward. The researchers used innovative methods to identify these candidates, and while more work is needed to confirm them, the possibilities are thrilling. Just imagine the extreme environments these planets could have—it's like science fiction becoming reality!
