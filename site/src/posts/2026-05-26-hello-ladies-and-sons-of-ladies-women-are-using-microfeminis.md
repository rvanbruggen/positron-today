So, have you heard about microfeminisms? They're these little, everyday actions that women are taking to challenge gender norms and promote equality. Tori Dunlap, a Seattle-based author, has sparked a fun TikTok trend where women share their quirky ways of practicing microfeminism, like greeting couples with the woman's name first. It's all about flipping the script and pushing back against everyday sexism in a light-hearted way, which is just brilliant!
