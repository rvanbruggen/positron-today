I’m absolutely enchanted by the Slovenian Riviera, where the lush hills meet the sparkling sea! Riding through the Dragonja valley, I’m greeted by stunning views of salt pans and vibrant landscapes. The local farm experience at Kmetija Medljan is a delight, especially with Marina’s delicious home-cooked meals. Exploring the charming town of Piran, with its rich Venetian history and culinary treasures, adds to the magic of this hidden gem on the Adriatic coast.
