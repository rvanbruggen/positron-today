So, this is one of those moments worth pausing for. Pope Leo used Easter to send a powerful message to the world - a call for peace paired with a warning that we risk becoming dangerously indifferent to violence. And here's the thing: that mix of hope and urgency is exactly what can shake us out of our comfort zones. Which brings me to what I find most striking - he's not just preaching, he's holding up a mirror to a world that sometimes forgets to feel. Good to know that kind of voice is out there making itself heard.
