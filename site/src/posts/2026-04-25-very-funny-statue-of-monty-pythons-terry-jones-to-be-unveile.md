A delightful statue of Terry Jones, the beloved Monty Python member, has just been unveiled in his hometown of Colwyn Bay. This bronze tribute captures him as the 'nude organist' from the show, a character that surely brings a smile to fans' faces. Fellow Python members, including Sir Michael Palin, attended the ceremony, sharing fond memories and laughter. It's a wonderful way to honor Terry's legacy and remind everyone of the joy he brought to comedy.
