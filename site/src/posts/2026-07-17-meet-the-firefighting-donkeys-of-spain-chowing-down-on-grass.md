So, have you heard about the amazing donkeys in Spain that are helping to prevent wildfires? In Doñana National Park, these gentle giants graze on grass and shrubs, turning potential fire fuel into food. It’s fascinating how they work better than sheep and goats, and they’re low-impact too! This innovative approach is being adopted in other regions of Spain, showing just how effective these happy little donkeys can be.
