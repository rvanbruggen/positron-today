It's amazing to see how more than a century later, Einstein's general theory of relativity continues to shine! Researchers have achieved a groundbreaking measurement of frame-dragging, confirming Einstein's predictions about how massive objects like Earth influence spacetime. This study, using data from the LARES-2 satellite, has improved measurements by over ten times, which is a huge leap in physics. It's not just a win for Einstein but also helps refine our understanding of gravity and its alternatives.
