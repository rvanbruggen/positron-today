It's fantastic news for conservationists as North Carolina's red wolf population is on the rise! Four litters of adorable red wolf pups have been born this year, with many surviving their early months. This comeback is a testament to the hard work of programs dedicated to their recovery. With new funding for wildlife crossings, there’s real hope for these magnificent creatures to thrive in the wild.
