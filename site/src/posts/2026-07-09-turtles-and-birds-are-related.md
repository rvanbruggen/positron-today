So, researchers have made an exciting discovery that turtles are actually related to birds and crocodiles! This revelation comes from a study published in Current Biology, which analyzed fossils from the time when these species evolved. It's fascinating to think that turtles developed shortly after a massive extinction event 252 million years ago. The study also highlights the unique evolutionary path of early turtles, like Pappochelys, which had a shell that started forming on the underside. This new perspective really changes how we view these animals and their connections in the animal kingdom!
