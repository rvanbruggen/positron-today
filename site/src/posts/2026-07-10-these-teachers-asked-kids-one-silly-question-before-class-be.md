It's amazing how a simple question can transform a classroom! At Martin Luther King, Jr. Middle School, teachers start each day by asking students about their favorite pizza topping or what makes them happy. This small change has led to a 50% drop in behavior problems and improved attendance. It really shows the power of kindness and connection in education.
