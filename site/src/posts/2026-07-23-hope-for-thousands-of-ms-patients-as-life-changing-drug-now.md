This is such exciting news! The NHS in England is now offering fampridine, the first drug that helps people with multiple sclerosis walk more easily. Aysen Slack, who previously had to pay for it privately, expressed how this decision will make a huge difference in her life. With up to 5,000 people set to benefit each year, this could truly be a game changer for many who struggle with mobility. It’s wonderful to see advancements that allow people to live more independently and confidently.
