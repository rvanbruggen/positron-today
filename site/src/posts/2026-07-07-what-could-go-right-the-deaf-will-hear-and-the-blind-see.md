It's incredible to think that over 100 people in the U.S. who were once completely blind can now see, thanks to the groundbreaking gene therapy Luxturna. This treatment, which replaces a faulty gene, has allowed children to live more normal lives, attending school and even getting driver’s licenses. And let's not forget about Otarmeni, the first gene therapy for genetic deafness, which is also making waves! While these advancements are monumental, the high costs of these treatments highlight the need for more accessible options in the future.
