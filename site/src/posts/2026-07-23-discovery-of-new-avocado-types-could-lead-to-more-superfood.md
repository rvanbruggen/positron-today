Scientists have uncovered a treasure trove of avocado diversity in Nicaragua’s rainforests, which could help diversify our food supply and bolster sustainable agriculture. This research highlights the genetic links between local avocados and those from South America, revealing a rich history of hybridization. The findings suggest that these unique varieties could be key to protecting commercial avocados from diseases that threaten the industry. It's exciting to think about how this discovery could lead to more resilient and nutritious superfood options for everyone!
