It's fantastic to see that 82 percent of young women with a non-Western background in Denmark are obtaining vocational qualifications! This new analysis shows that the education gap has shrunk significantly over the last 20 years, and among these young women, it's completely closed. In fact, they're outpacing their Danish counterparts slightly, with 81 percent achieving similar qualifications. While there's still a gap across genders, this progress is truly inspiring and highlights the positive changes in education access.
