Can you believe it? A group of students from Italy's University of Pisa has just built the largest paper airplane ever, breaking a record that stood for 13 years! Their incredible creation has a wingspan of over 65 feet and glided an impressive 200 feet during its flight. It all started as a fun project between lessons, showing that with creativity and teamwork, even a simple piece of paper can achieve greatness. This achievement not only showcases their engineering skills but also brings a sense of pride back to Italy!
