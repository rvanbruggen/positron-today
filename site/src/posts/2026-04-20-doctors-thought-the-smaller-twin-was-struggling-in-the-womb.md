But after seeking a second opinion, Poppy turned out to be perfectly healthy and had actually been sending distress signals to save her sister, Winnie. Now, at six years old, both girls are thriving, with Poppy keeping a watchful eye on her sister. It's a beautiful reminder of the bond between siblings!
