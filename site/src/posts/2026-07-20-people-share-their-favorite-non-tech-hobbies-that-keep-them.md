Digital fatigue can really take a toll on our happiness, but there’s a delightful way to escape it—analog hobbies! From painting to gardening, these activities not only boost our creativity but also help clear our minds. Many people discovered the joy of tech-free hobbies during the pandemic, and it’s clear that the need to unplug is still strong. So, if you're looking for some inspiration, why not dive into something like journaling or even aquascaping? It’s all about finding that satisfying, hands-on experience!
