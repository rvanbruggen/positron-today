What a remarkable story - a 14-year-old American boy named Andrew who had been missing for three years has finally been found, and it happened almost by accident when border alarms were triggered. So the authorities were alerted through what seems to have been a chance encounter at a border crossing, which led them directly to the missing teenager. Now, after such an incredibly long search, this discovery brings real hope and relief to everyone involved - his family must be overjoyed to have him back. And here's the thing: these kinds of cases that stretch on for years often feel hopeless, which makes this unexpected breakthrough all the more wonderful.
