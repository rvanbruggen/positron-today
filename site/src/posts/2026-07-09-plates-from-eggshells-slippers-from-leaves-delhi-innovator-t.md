Isn't it amazing how creativity can turn waste into something beautiful? Midushi Kochhar, a talented designer from Delhi, has taken her passion for unique materials and created a brand called YLEM. She uses eggshells to make stunning plates and slippers from leaves, showing us that everything in nature has value. With her innovative approach, she's not just making products, but also inspiring a new way of thinking about waste and sustainability.
