It's fascinating to see how biologists like Kathleen Hunt are uncovering the secrets of marine mammals through their baleen and other unique materials. By analyzing these samples, they can learn about the whales' life histories, stress levels, and even their reproductive patterns. This research is crucial, especially for endangered species like the North Atlantic right whale. It's a game-changer in understanding these elusive creatures and their environments, and it feels like we're just scratching the surface of what we can discover.
