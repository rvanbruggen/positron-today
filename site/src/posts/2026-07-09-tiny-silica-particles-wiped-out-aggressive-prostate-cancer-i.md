Researchers have developed tiny silica nanoparticles that can destroy prostate tumors and activate the immune system to fight cancer. In mouse models, these particles led to complete tumor remissions, showing promise for future human trials. Made from natural silica, they attack cancer cells while sparing healthy ones. This innovative approach could redefine cancer treatment by combining direct tumor destruction with immune activation.
