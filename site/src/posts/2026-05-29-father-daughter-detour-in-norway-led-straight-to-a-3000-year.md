So, Tormod Fjeld and his daughter Ada stumbled upon an incredible discovery while driving in Norway. They found detailed Bronze Age rock carvings of ships and even human footprints, which are quite rare in Norwegian rock art. This site, made of sandstone, is unique and shows the artistry of a community from 3,000 years ago. It's a reminder of how much history is still out there waiting to be uncovered!
