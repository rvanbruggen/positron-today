So, researchers have made an exciting discovery about mRNA cancer vaccines that could change the game! They found that even without a key immune cell, these vaccines still triggered a strong response against tumors. Instead, another immune cell stepped up to help fight cancer, which is just fascinating! This insight could lead to even more effective cancer vaccines in the future, making a real difference in treatment options.
