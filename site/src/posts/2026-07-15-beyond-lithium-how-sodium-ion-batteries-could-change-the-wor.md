So, the lithium-ion battery has been our go-to power source for ages, but sodium-ion batteries are stepping into the spotlight! They promise to be cheaper, safer, and more eco-friendly, which is super exciting. This year, CATL, the largest battery producer, is set to mass-produce these sodium batteries, and that could really shake things up. With advancements happening faster than expected, it looks like sodium-ion technology might just revolutionize the battery market and help drive down prices even further.
