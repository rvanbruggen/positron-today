NASA is gearing up for its Artemis III mission in 2027, and it's introducing an exciting project called MoonFall. This mission will deploy four drones to explore the moon's South Pole, scouting for potential landing sites for future astronauts. Each drone is designed to gather high-resolution images and will even survive the harsh lunar night. With this innovative technology, we’re getting closer to establishing a permanent presence on the moon!
