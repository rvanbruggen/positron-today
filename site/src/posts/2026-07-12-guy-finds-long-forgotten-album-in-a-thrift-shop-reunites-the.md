Imagine finding a lost album from a band that hasn't been heard in decades! That's exactly what happened when Marcus Pollard stumbled upon a damaged vinyl of The Glass Cage. After two years of searching, he finally connected with band member Norman Roth, who was thrilled to hear their music again. Now, thanks to Pollard's dedication, their album 'Where Did the Sunshine Go?' is set to be released in 2026, bringing joy to both the band and their fans.
