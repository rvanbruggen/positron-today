So, have you ever found yourself halfway up a mountain, questioning your life choices? That’s what ‘Type 2’ fun is all about—it’s the tough stuff that feels miserable in the moment but brings joy later on. Engaging in these challenging activities can actually boost your mental resilience and provide a deeper sense of accomplishment. It’s a fantastic way to break out of your routine and discover more about yourself while embracing discomfort.
