It's an exciting time for biodiversity science as we uncover more about the planet's life than ever before! Despite the ongoing threats to biodiversity, recent advances in technology, like affordable DNA sequencing, are helping scientists describe new species at record rates. From the depths of the ocean to the highlands of Angola, researchers are exploring previously uncharted territories. And with digitization and AI on the rise, we might soon see a breakthrough in plant taxonomy as well!
