How cool is this? A British couple has spent two decades turning trees into beautiful chairs, blending nature with art! They started their furniture orchard in 2006, and it takes about 6-9 years to grow each chair. Gavin Munro's innovative idea came to him during his childhood hospital stays, and now they want to share this unique craft with others. It's not just chairs—they're also experimenting with growing tables and lamps, making furniture that’s truly one-of-a-kind!
