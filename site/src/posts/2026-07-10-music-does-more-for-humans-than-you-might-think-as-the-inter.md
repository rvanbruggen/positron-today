So, there's this fantastic exhibition called ‘MUSIC!’ at the Museon-Omniversum in The Hague that’s all about the joy and impact of music, especially for kids! It’s not just a boring display – kids can actually create their own songs, learn dance moves, and even explore how music affects their brains. The interactive pavilions make it super engaging, and there’s even a giant projected record on the floor that shares fun music history facts. Honestly, it’s a wonderful way for children to discover the magic of music while having a blast!
