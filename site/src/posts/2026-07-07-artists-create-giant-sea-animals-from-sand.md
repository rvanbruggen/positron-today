On the beautiful island of Rab in Croatia, artists are crafting impressive sand sculptures of marine creatures during the 15th Festival of Sand Sculptures. It's amazing to see a monk seal, a turtle, and a giant squid take shape right on the beach! The event transforms the sandy shore into a vibrant open-air gallery, showcasing the artists' teamwork and creativity. What's really cool is that these sculptures are temporary, shaped by nature itself before they return to the beach.
