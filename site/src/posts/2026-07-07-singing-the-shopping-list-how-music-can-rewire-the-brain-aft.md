In a remarkable journey, Naresh Shanbhag transformed his life after a stroke with the help of music therapy. Initially skeptical, he found joy and progress in singing his shopping list, which helped him regain his speech. The innovative work at Bengaluru’s music cognition lab showcases how engaging with music can rewire the brain and aid recovery. This approach not only fosters communication but also brings a sense of achievement and independence to patients like Shanbhag.
