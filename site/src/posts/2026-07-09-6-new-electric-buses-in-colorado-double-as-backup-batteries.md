How cool is this? A Colorado school district has introduced a fleet of electric buses that not only transport kids but also serve as backup batteries for the local power grid! After dropping off students, these buses can release stored energy during peak demand times, helping to stabilize the grid. This innovative approach not only supports environmental goals but also saves money in the long run. It's a win-win for the community and the planet!
