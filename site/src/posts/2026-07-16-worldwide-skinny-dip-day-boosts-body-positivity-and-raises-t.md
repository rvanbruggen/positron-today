Every year, people around the globe celebrate International Skinny Dip Day, and it’s all for a fantastic cause! This event not only promotes body positivity but has also raised over $60,000 for the Fistula Foundation since its inception. Participants report feeling liberated and more confident, which is just wonderful! It’s amazing to see how a quirky tradition can make such a big difference in the lives of women in need of surgeries.
