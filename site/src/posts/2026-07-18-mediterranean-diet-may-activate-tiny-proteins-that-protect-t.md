So, it turns out that the Mediterranean diet might do more than just keep our taste buds happy. A recent study found that this diet can boost levels of tiny proteins in our cells that help protect our hearts and brains. Researchers discovered that older adults who closely followed this eating pattern had higher levels of these microproteins, which are linked to better health outcomes. It's fascinating to think that what we eat can influence our cellular health and aging in such a significant way!
