So, Aakarsh Shamanur, a social entrepreneur from Bengaluru, has come up with a brilliant idea to help women street vendors. He created Be Po-Lite, which produces portable solar lamps that allow these vendors to work after dark. This innovation not only lights up their stalls but also helps them save money and charge their phones. Aakarsh dreams of expanding this initiative across India, bringing light and opportunity to many more women.
