So, on July 8 at around 11:10 GMT, a whopping 99 percent of the world's population will bask in sunlight all at once! This amazing moment will last just about a minute, with around 8.2 billion people enjoying daylight or twilight together. It's fascinating to think that while most of the world is lit up, only a small fraction will be in darkness. This phenomenon happens for about 60 days each year, but July 8 has gained popularity thanks to social media buzz.
