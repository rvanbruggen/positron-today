So, here's the exciting news: a new study from University College London shows that engaging in arts and culture can actually extend your life by about 4%! This means that if you love going to museums, attending concerts, or even just reading a good book, you're not just enjoying yourself but also boosting your longevity. The research highlights how creative activities can slow biological aging and improve overall health, similar to exercise. It’s a fantastic reminder for all of us to embrace our artistic sides and make time for the things we love.
