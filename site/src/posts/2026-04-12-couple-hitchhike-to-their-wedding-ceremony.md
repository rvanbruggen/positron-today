What a story! Chloe and George Gardner found themselves in a bit of a pickle on their wedding day when they couldn't get a taxi to the Brighton register office. After being turned down by multiple Uber drivers, they decided to walk along the promenade, hoping to make it in time. Luckily, a friendly driver named Reggie stopped to help them out, and they hitched a ride just in time for their ceremony. Now, they can look back and laugh at their unexpected adventure!
