Imagine having a device that can tell you if you're burning fat just by breathing into it! This new handheld breathalyser measures acetone levels in real time, helping people manage their weight loss more effectively. It's a game changer for those on low-carb diets or high-intensity workouts, as it provides instant feedback without the need for clinic visits. Researchers are excited about its potential, although more testing is needed to confirm its accuracy with a larger group of people.
