So, astronomers are scratching their heads over a new chemical signature found on both Pluto and Titan, thanks to the James Webb Space Telescope. This signature doesn’t match anything in existing databases, which is super exciting! Researchers think it could be a compound never seen before, or perhaps a mix of materials we haven't studied yet. The fact that both these distant worlds, so different from each other, show the same signal adds to the mystery, making it a thrilling time for planetary science.
