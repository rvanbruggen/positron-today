It's amazing to see how wildlife crossings are becoming a global priority! These structures, like underpasses and overpasses, are crucial for reconnecting animal habitats that roads have fragmented. In Colorado, for example, new crossings are expected to reduce roadkill by 90% and help wildlife thrive. This movement not only protects animals but also enhances public safety and biodiversity, which is something we can all get behind!
