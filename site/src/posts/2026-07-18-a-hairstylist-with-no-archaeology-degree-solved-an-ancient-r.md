Isn't it amazing how a hairstylist can unravel ancient mysteries? Janet Stephens, a Baltimore-based hairdresser, challenged the long-held belief that Roman hairstyles were achieved with wigs. Through her hands-on experiments and a deep dive into historical texts, she discovered that the key to these intricate styles lay in the use of needle and thread. This groundbreaking revelation not only changed the way we view Roman hairdressing but also highlights the importance of looking at history from different perspectives.
