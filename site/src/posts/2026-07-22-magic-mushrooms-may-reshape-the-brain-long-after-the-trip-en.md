Researchers have found that a single dose of psilocybin from magic mushrooms can lead to lasting changes in brain activity, detectable for up to a month. This study, involving healthy adults, suggests that increased brain 'entropy' during the psychedelic experience may enhance emotional insight and well-being. Interestingly, participants who experienced more insight reported feeling better a month later. This research opens up exciting possibilities for understanding how psychedelics could be used therapeutically for mental health.
