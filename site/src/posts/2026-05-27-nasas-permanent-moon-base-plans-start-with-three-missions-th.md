So, NASA is gearing up for some exciting lunar missions this year! They’ve announced three Moon Base missions that will lead to a crewed Artemis landing in 2028. The first mission will use Blue Origin’s lander to study the Moon’s surface, while the second will deliver a rover to help with future lunar operations. And here's the thing – these missions are just the beginning of a whole series planned for the future!
