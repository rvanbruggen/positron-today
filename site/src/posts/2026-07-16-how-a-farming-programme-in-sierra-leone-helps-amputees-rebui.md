I find it inspiring how a farming programme in Sierra Leone is transforming the lives of amputees. Founded by Mambud Samai, this initiative teaches sustainable farming skills, allowing participants to grow food and create small businesses. After years of struggle, many are finding independence and dignity through agriculture. It's amazing to see how this community is coming together and thriving, proving that resilience can lead to new beginnings.
