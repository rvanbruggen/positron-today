So, this article really dives into how collaboration in nature is often overshadowed by the idea of competition. Science journalist Rowan Hooper highlights that working together has been crucial in shaping our world. For instance, he shares the fascinating relationship between the bobtail squid and the specialized bacteria it relies on. This partnership is a perfect example of how cooperation can lead to success in nature.
