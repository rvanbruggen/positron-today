I'm really excited about the Martin Family Legacy Garden, which is set to be Canada's first dementia-inclusive park opening in Calgary! This park will feature sensory gardens, wide pathways, and shaded seating, all designed with the needs of people with dementia in mind. It’s amazing to see how the community came together, involving researchers and families to create a space that truly supports everyone. Plus, it’s conveniently located near seniors residences, making it accessible for many. This initiative represents a significant step towards inclusivity in our society!
