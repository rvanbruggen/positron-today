What a wild rescue story! In Stoke-on-Trent, a pony was discovered completely trapped inside a tractor tire, and no one knew how it got there. Thankfully, the local residents quickly called the RSPCA, who managed to free the scared little guy without any injuries. After a delicate operation, the pony was released and soon started feeding again, proving that sometimes, teamwork really does save the day!
