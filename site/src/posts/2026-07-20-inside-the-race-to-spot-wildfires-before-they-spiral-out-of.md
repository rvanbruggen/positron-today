Wildfires have become a growing threat, especially after the devastating 'Big Burn' of 1910, which is still the largest forest fire in U.S. history. Climate change and urban expansion are making these fires more frequent and intense, causing significant loss of life and property. Fortunately, advancements in technology are finally improving our ability to detect these fires early. Experts like Neal Driscoll are optimistic that new tools will help us better manage and respond to wildfires before they escalate.
