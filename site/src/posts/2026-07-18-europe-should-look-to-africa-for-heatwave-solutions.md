As Europe grapples with dangerous heatwaves, it's crucial to learn from Africa, where communities have been adapting to climate challenges for generations. African innovations in architecture and urban greening offer valuable lessons for European cities facing extreme heat. For instance, the climate-smart building designs from West Africa and Freetown's tree-planting initiative provide practical solutions to combat urban heat. By embracing these approaches, Europe can enhance its resilience and create healthier environments for its most vulnerable populations.
