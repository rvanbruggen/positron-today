Hey, creative minds! NASA is inviting artists, storytellers, and even songwriters to join them in sharing their mission stories, which is just fantastic. They’re looking for proposals for projects related to exciting missions like Artemis III and the journey to Mars. Plus, there's a fun art challenge called Moon Joy June coming up, where you can express your creativity based on weekly prompts! It's all about having fun and sharing art, so don’t worry about competition—just let your imagination soar.
