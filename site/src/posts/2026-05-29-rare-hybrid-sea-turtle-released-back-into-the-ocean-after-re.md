A unique hybrid sea turtle named Earl Grey has been given a second chance at life in the ocean! This special turtle, a mix of a Loggerhead and a Kemp's ridley, was rescued from a beach in Massachusetts and has now been successfully released back into the wild. After a slight delay due to pre-release complications, the team at the Georgia Sea Turtle Center ensured Earl Grey was ready for his big adventure. It's fascinating to think about how hybridization might boost genetic diversity in sea turtle populations!
