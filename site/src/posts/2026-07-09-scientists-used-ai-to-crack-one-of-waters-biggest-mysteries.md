Water is such a fascinating substance, right? It expands when it freezes, which is pretty unusual for a liquid. Researchers at the University of Osaka have harnessed AI to better understand the microscopic structure of supercooled water, which behaves strangely under certain conditions. By comparing different methods of describing water's structure, they've made significant strides in uncovering the secrets behind its unique properties.
