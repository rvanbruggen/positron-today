Designed to assist those with mobility issues, it autonomously navigates to the user, making life a bit easier for everyone involved. With features like a built-in bidet and self-cleaning capabilities, it’s not just about convenience but also hygiene. I mean, who wouldn’t want a toilet that comes to them?
