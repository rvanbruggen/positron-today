North Carolina is making waves with its impressive $10.2 million investment in wildlife corridors, aiming to connect habitats and drastically reduce vehicle collisions. A groundbreaking project is the construction of a unique land bridge at Stecoah Gap, designed for both hikers and local wildlife. This innovative bridge features a mirrored design, ensuring safe passage for bears and rattlesnakes while keeping human hikers secure. It's thrilling to see how this project will enhance safety and connectivity in the stunning landscapes of the Great Smoky Mountains.
