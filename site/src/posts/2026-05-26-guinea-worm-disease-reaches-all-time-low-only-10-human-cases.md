Wow, can you believe it? Only 10 human cases of Guinea worm disease were reported in 2025, marking the lowest number ever! This incredible achievement brings us closer to eradicating this ancient disease, which has seen a 33% decline from the previous year. The Carter Center's efforts, supported by communities and volunteers, have truly made a difference, reducing cases by over 99.99% since 1986. It's a testament to hope and hard work, and we’re all cheering for a future free of this suffering!
