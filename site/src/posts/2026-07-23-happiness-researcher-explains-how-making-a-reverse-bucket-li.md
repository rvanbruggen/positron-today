So, Arthur C. Brooks, a happiness researcher, has discovered that happiness isn't just about achieving goals. Instead, he suggests creating a 'reverse bucket list' where you write down your desires and then cross them off, freeing yourself from attachment. This approach helps you realize that true satisfaction comes from wanting less rather than having more. It's a fascinating shift in perspective that can lead to a more fulfilling life.
