So, it turns out that hamsters running on wheels isn't just a quirky behavior! Researchers discovered that even wild mice enjoy running on wheels, suggesting it's not just about boredom. The excitement comes from a dopamine boost, making it a joyful activity rather than a sign of stress. This fascinating insight into animal behavior shows that even in captivity, hamsters are just having a great time!
