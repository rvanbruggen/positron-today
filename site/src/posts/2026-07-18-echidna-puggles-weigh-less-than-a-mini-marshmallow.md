So, have you heard about the adorable baby echidnas, or puggles, being raised at the San Diego Zoo Safari Park? These little ones are super needy and weigh about as much as half a mini marshmallow when they hatch! Kathryn, the short-beaked echidna, has given birth to two puggles, and one is being hand-raised by dedicated staff. It's fascinating to see how they care for these tiny creatures, monitoring their health and feeding them regularly to ensure they thrive.
