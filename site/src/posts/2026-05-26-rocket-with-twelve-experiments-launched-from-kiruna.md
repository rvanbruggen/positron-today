So, a new research rocket is set to launch from the Esrange space base near Kiruna, and it’s carrying twelve exciting experiments! These experiments will explore physical, biological, and medical processes in microgravity for over six minutes. It's fascinating to think about how these conditions can reveal effects that we usually can't see under normal gravity. Plus, one of the experiments comes from the emerging space nation of Saint Kitts and Nevis, which is just fantastic!
