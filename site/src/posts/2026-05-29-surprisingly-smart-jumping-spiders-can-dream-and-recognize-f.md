Jumping spiders are proving to be surprisingly intelligent despite their tiny brains. Recent studies show they can dream during sleep, exhibiting rapid eye movements similar to REM sleep. They also have impressive memory skills, recognizing other spiders and remembering places they've been. This intelligence makes them popular pets, as they are curious and interact with humans, charming even those who fear spiders.
