A Quebec firm has developed an innovative cyanide-free method for gold extraction that could greatly reduce environmental impact. Dundee Sustainable Technologies has also created a process to turn harmful arsenic into inert glass, addressing a major industry concern. This technology allows mining companies to operate more sustainably while meeting strict regulatory requirements. With promising results already seen in tests, this approach could revolutionize the mining industry for the better.
