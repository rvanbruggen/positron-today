What a fantastic moment for Spain and Gibraltar! The last border fence in western Europe has finally come down, thanks to a new post-Brexit deal that eliminates border checks for residents and workers. This agreement, which took years to negotiate, promises a new era of cooperation and prosperity for both sides. It's incredible to think about how this will ease daily life for thousands of people crossing the border every day.
