Captain DC Sekhar spent nearly two decades cleaning oil tanks, facing dangerous conditions that could incapacitate workers in moments. Frustrated by the risks of manual cleaning, he envisioned a robot that could safely handle the sludge without putting people in harm's way. This idea led to the creation of BetaTANK Robotics, which now develops robots designed to clean petroleum storage tanks. It's a remarkable innovation that not only protects workers but also modernizes an outdated industry practice.
