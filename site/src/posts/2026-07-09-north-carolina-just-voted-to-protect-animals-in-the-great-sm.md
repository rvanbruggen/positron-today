Wow, North Carolina is making waves with a groundbreaking $10.2 million investment in wildlife crossings! This funding will help create safe passages for animals, reducing dangerous wildlife-vehicle collisions. It's fantastic to see lawmakers prioritizing both wildlife and public safety, especially in such a biodiverse area like the Great Smoky Mountains. This effort, backed by a coalition of dedicated organizations, is a huge step towards reconnecting habitats and ensuring a safer future for both animals and people.
