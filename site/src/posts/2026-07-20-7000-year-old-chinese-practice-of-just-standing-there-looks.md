Isn't it fascinating how something as simple as standing can be so beneficial? Jules Horn, a former model, discovered the ancient practice of Zhan Zhuang, which involves standing still for 20 minutes a day. This practice not only strengthens the body but also calms the mind, challenging us to focus and breathe intentionally. Medical experts agree that this standing meditation can help alleviate aches, boost energy, and even improve mood—who knew standing could be so powerful?
