Bengaluru has turned a problematic drain into a vibrant green corridor, showcasing an innovative approach to water management. The K100 waterway, once a sewage channel, now thrives with life as it helps manage flooding and replenish groundwater. This restoration project not only cleans the canal but also revives a centuries-old system that once sustained the city. It's a fantastic example of how urban spaces can be transformed to benefit both people and nature.
