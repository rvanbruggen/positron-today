In a world dominated by negative news, many people are stepping back from traditional media, feeling overwhelmed and guilty. This editorial explores the reasons behind this trend, emphasizing how human psychology and media incentives skew our perception of reality. The Positron Today project aims to highlight positive news stories that often go unnoticed. By filtering out negativity, it seeks to balance the narrative and remind us that good things are happening all around us.
