In a world dominated by negative news, many people are stepping back from traditional media, feeling overwhelmed and guilty. This editorial explores how human psychology and media incentives skew our perception towards negativity. The Positron Today project aims to highlight positive stories that often get overlooked. By filtering out negativity, it seeks to provide a more balanced view of the world.
