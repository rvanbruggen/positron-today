In a world saturated with negativity, many people are turning away from the news, feeling overwhelmed and guilty for not staying informed. The Positron Today project aims to highlight the positive stories that often get overshadowed by negative headlines. By filtering out negativity, it provides a refreshing perspective on the world, showing that good news is not rare but rather underreported. This initiative seeks to balance the narrative and encourage a healthier relationship with the news.
