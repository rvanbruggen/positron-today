Isn't it fascinating how ancient wisdom can guide us today? In India, the arrival of the monsoon has always signaled the perfect time to plant trees, a practice rooted in generations of observation. This natural timing not only helps saplings thrive but also strengthens local ecosystems by preventing soil erosion and conserving water. With native species like neem, jamun, and mango, communities are continuing a tradition that benefits both people and the planet.
