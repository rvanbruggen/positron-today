At The Long Table in Stroud, a pay-as-you-can restaurant, the atmosphere is buzzing with life and community spirit. People from all walks of life come together to share meals, with no judgment on what they can afford. The concept of 'radical hospitality' allows guests to pay what they can, fostering a unique social mix and encouraging connections. It's not just about food; it's about building relationships and supporting each other during tough times.
