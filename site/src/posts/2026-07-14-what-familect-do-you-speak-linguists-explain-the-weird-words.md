Isn't it fascinating how every family has its own unique language? This concept, called 'familect,' is all about those quirky words and phrases that only your loved ones understand. Linguist Cynthia Gordon has spent years studying these private dialects, revealing how they help strengthen family bonds and create a sense of belonging. It's like a secret code that deepens trust and even helps mend arguments without a word of apology.
