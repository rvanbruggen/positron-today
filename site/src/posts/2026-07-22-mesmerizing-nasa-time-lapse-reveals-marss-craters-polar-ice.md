So, NASA's Psyche spacecraft has taken a scenic route to a metal-rich asteroid, capturing stunning images of Mars along the way. The time-lapse video showcases the planet's craters, gleaming polar ice caps, and vast deserts, all while using Mars's gravity for a boost. It's amazing to see how the spacecraft's multispectral imager performed brilliantly during this flyby, even spotting Mars's moons from afar. With three more years until it reaches its destination, this mission is already shaping up to be an exciting journey into our solar system!
