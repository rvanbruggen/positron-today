Kelsey Pfendler's journey from California to Hawaii was nothing short of extraordinary. Not only did she become the first American woman to row solo across 2,400 miles, but she also shattered multiple records along the way. Upon reaching land, she shared powerful advice about tackling our own 'big, hard, scary things' and emphasized that starting is often the hardest part. Her experience highlights the emotional complexities of achieving such a monumental goal, especially when it's tied to a cause like mental health support.
