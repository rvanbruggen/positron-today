So, a seven-year-old girl named Nina Evans made an incredible discovery on Felixstowe beach – a bone fragment that could be up to half a million years old! While searching for shark teeth with her family, she stumbled upon this piece that an AI app suggests might belong to a mammoth. Professor Ben Garrod, an evolutionary biologist, mentioned that it could also come from other large mammals, making it a fascinating find. It's amazing to think that this bone could have once been part of a creature roaming the area when ancient humans were around!
