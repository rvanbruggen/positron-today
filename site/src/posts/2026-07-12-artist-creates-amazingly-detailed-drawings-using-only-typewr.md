So, there's this incredible artist named James Cook from London who creates stunning artworks using just a vintage typewriter! He types over a million letters and symbols to craft his masterpieces, and his latest piece, depicting a Wimbledon match, took him around 378 hours to complete. It's amazing how he incorporates whimsical elements into his work, like a man dressed as a giant strawberry! His journey from a school project to becoming a full-time artist is truly inspiring.
