The Flemish government has decided to build at least 56,000 extra social housing units by 2042. The goal is to tackle the severe housing crisis in Flanders, where many families struggle to afford a home. Minister Hans Bonte calls for a mass investment in social housing and emphasizes the importance of every community's contribution to this aim. With a budget of over €1 billion per year, the Flemish government aims to accelerate the construction process while keeping interest rates low.
