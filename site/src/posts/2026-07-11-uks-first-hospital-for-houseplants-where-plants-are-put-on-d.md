How exciting is it that the UK now has a hospital dedicated to houseplants? Located in Edinburgh, the Hilda Houseplant Hospital offers expert care for indoor plants, including repotting and pest treatment. Rosanna Costello, the founder, understands the emotional connection people have with their plants and provides tailored care based on each plant's needs. It's a fantastic resource for both novice and experienced plant lovers looking to keep their green friends thriving.
