So, scientists have stumbled upon the faintest planet ever captured by a ground-based telescope, and it’s been hiding in plain sight for over a decade! This exoplanet, named Beta Pictoris d, orbits the same star as its brighter neighbor, Beta Pictoris b. What’s really exciting is that the method used to discover this dim planet might help us find other elusive exoplanets out there. It’s a fantastic reminder of how much we still have to learn about our universe!
