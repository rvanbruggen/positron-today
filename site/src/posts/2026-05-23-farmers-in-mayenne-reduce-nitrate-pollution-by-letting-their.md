In Mayenne, a group of farmers is making a remarkable shift towards cleaner agriculture by allowing their cows to graze more. Instead of relying on corn and soy for feed, they are optimizing their land use, which has numerous benefits for the environment. This approach not only reduces nitrate pollution but also enhances the well-being of the animals. It's inspiring to see how these farmers are working together to create a sustainable future for their communities.
