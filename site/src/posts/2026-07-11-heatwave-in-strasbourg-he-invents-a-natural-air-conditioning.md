So, while France is facing another heatwave, an engineer named Damien Marcel has come up with a brilliant natural solution to keep homes cool. He’s developed a system that uses a rainwater tank buried underground to cool the air before it enters the house. This innovative method not only lowers indoor temperatures by a few degrees but also consumes significantly less energy than traditional air conditioning. It’s exciting to think that this could be expanded to larger urban areas in the future!
