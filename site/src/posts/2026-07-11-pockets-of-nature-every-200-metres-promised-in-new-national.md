Ireland is buzzing with excitement over a new national plan aimed at creating 'pockets' of natural habitat every 200 metres to support our precious pollinators. This initiative, part of the third All-Ireland Pollinator Plan, is designed to help combat the decline of wild bees and other pollinators. With a focus on engaging farmers, businesses, and schools, the plan aims to enhance biodiversity and ensure food sustainability. It's a fantastic step forward for the environment, and I can't wait to see how it unfolds!
