It's so exciting to see languages making a comeback in schools! Thanks to a fresh approach led by Cardiff University, students are diving into diverse languages like Mandarin and Japanese while exploring culture and identity. This innovative mentoring program is all about understanding different perspectives, rather than just memorizing vocabulary. With a significant rise in language uptake, it's clear that this method is inspiring a new generation of language learners.
