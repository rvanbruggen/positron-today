So, it turns out that the key to a lasting friendship isn't about shared interests or how long you've known each other. According to recent studies, honesty is the most desired trait in a friend, topping the list by a wide margin. Interestingly, dishonesty is seen as the most undesirable quality, which makes sense, right? This means that those friends who give you the tough love might just be the ones who stick around for the long haul.
