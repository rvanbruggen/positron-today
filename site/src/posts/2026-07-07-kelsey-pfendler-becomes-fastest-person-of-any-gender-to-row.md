Kelsey Pfendler has just made history by becoming the fastest person of any gender to row solo from California to Hawaii, completing the journey in just 43 days! She set out from Monterey on May 21, aiming to inspire women and raise funds for the Whale Foundation. Not only did she break the women's record, but she also surpassed the men's record by more than a week. Her arrival in Honolulu was met with cheers from hundreds of supporters, celebrating her incredible achievement and the awareness she raised for the Grand Canyon river guiding community.
