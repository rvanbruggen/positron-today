It's fascinating to see how the world is experiencing a cultural renaissance with the great global museum boom. Over the past few decades, the number of museums has skyrocketed from 22,000 to an astonishing 104,000! This surge is not just about numbers; it's about democratizing access to art and culture across the globe. Countries like China and the UAE are leading the charge, opening new museums at an incredible pace, and even nations with fewer resources are getting in on the action. This is a truly exciting time for art lovers everywhere!
