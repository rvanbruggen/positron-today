In mid-April, the Lyrids meteor shower will light up the night sky, especially for those in the northern hemisphere. With 15 to 20 meteors per hour at its peak, it's a fantastic opportunity for astronomy lovers to enjoy a celestial show. Just find the constellation Lyra and the bright star Vega to locate the radiant point of the meteors. Remember to let your eyes adjust to the darkness for the best viewing experience!
