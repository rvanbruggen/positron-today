So, researchers in Hungary explored whether cats, dogs, or toddlers would help humans in distress. The study found that while all three species showed some concern, dogs and toddlers were more likely to jump in and help out. Cats, on the other hand, tended to only get involved if it served their own interests. This sparked a lively discussion online about the true nature of our feline friends and their unique ways of showing love.
