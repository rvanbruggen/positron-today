Jon James, a barber from Queensland, is making waves by bringing his hair-cutting workshops to the remote Mornington Island. He found a community eager to learn, quickly picking up skills and even helping him with a wedding party! The experience was not just about haircuts but also about building connections and hope in a place that truly welcomed him. With professional barber kits to share, James is excited to see the new barber boom flourish in this resilient community.
