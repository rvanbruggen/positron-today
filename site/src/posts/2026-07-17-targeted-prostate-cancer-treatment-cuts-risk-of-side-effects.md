A new study shows that focal therapy for prostate cancer is just as effective as traditional surgery or radiotherapy, but with significantly fewer side effects. This less invasive treatment uses high-intensity ultrasound or cryotherapy to target cancerous tissue. Researchers followed nearly 3,500 men for a decade and found excellent long-term outcomes, which could push for wider availability of this therapy. It's exciting to think that this could change the way prostate cancer is treated across the UK!
