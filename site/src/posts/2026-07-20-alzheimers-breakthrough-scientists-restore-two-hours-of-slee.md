Imagine this: researchers have discovered that the immune cells in our brains, called microglia, are the main culprits behind sleep loss in Alzheimer's disease. By temporarily eliminating these cells in mice, they managed to restore over two hours of sleep each day! This finding is a game changer, suggesting that the issue isn't just the amyloid plaques we often hear about. It opens up exciting new avenues for treatment that could really help those suffering from this condition.
