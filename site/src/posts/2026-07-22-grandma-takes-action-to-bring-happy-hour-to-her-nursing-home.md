Anita LeBrun, an 82-year-old resident, fought for the right to enjoy happy hour in her nursing home, and she won! After discovering that her facility couldn't serve alcohol due to licensing issues, she took her concerns to lawmakers. Thanks to her efforts, a new law now allows nursing homes in Minnesota to serve alcohol during social events, fostering connection and joy among residents. This change, aptly named the 'Grandparents’ Happy Hour' law, emphasizes that growing older shouldn't mean losing cherished traditions.
