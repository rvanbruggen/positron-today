Isn't it fascinating to think about what we would tell our younger selves? At McCallie School, sixth-grade boys recorded messages for their future selves, and seven years later, they got to listen to their own questions and answers. The boys asked about their favorite movies, hobbies, and even life lessons, creating a heartfelt connection across time. It's a beautiful reminder of growth and the importance of kindness, with the older boys offering sincere advice to their younger selves.
