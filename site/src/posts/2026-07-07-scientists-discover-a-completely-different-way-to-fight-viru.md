So, researchers have found a fascinating new way that sea anemones defend against viruses, which really shakes up our understanding of animal immunity. They discovered a protein called CARDIB that surprisingly suppresses the immune response instead of activating it, which is quite the twist! This suggests that evolution has crafted multiple strategies for fighting viruses across different species. It’s amazing how studying these ancient creatures can reveal such groundbreaking insights into immunity.
