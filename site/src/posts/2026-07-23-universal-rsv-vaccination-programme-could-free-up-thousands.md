So, here's the exciting news! Experts in New Zealand are advocating for a universal RSV vaccination programme that could significantly reduce hospitalisations among young children. This initiative could free up thousands of hospital beds each year, easing the strain on health services during the winter months. Countries like Australia have already seen fantastic results from similar programmes, with hospitalisations nearly halved. It's a great step towards improving health equity and protecting our little ones from serious complications.
