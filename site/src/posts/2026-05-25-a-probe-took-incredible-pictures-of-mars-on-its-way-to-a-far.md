So, the Psyche probe has just zoomed past Mars, snapping some stunning images of the red planet! This flyby was all about using Mars' gravity to speed up its journey to a metallic asteroid, and wow, did it deliver. The pictures show off the rugged Martian terrain and even hints of water ice, which is super exciting. With the probe now moving at 1,600 kilometers per hour, it's on track to reach Psyche by the summer of 2029!
