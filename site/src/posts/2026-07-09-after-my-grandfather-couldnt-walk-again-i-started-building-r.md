This inspiring story follows Robin Kanattu Thomas, who founded Astrek Innovations after witnessing his grandfather's struggle to walk post-surgery. The startup has developed a lower-limb robotic exoskeleton designed to aid rehabilitation, making advanced technology accessible in India. Robin's journey highlights the gap in rehabilitation support and the importance of local solutions. With years of research and a personal mission, Astrek is changing lives one step at a time.
