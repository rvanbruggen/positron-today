So, here's the exciting news from Zoo Planckendael! After almost 9 years, the Asian elephant May Tagu is pregnant, and this is a big deal for the species. With their population under threat, every birth counts, and the zoo is thrilled about this upcoming addition. Interestingly, the father, Kanvar, was thought to be unable to father a calf, but surprise! The pregnancy was confirmed through fecal samples, which is quite fascinating. If all goes well, we can expect the little one to arrive in the summer of 2027!
