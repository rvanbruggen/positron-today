Researchers in Uganda and Wales have found that catnip oil can effectively repel mosquitoes, presenting an exciting alternative to traditional sprays. During tests, a 6% concentration of catnip oil proved just as effective as DEET, while a 2% concentration was only slightly less effective. This discovery is particularly valuable for local farmers in Uganda, where commercial repellents can be too expensive. The goal is to create a locally produced, effective repellent that is affordable for everyone.
