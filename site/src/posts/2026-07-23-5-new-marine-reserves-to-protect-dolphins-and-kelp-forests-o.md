I'm really excited to share that New Zealand is taking a big step forward with the creation of 5 new marine reserves! These areas will protect vital habitats for amazing species like Hector's dolphin and yellow-eyed penguins. What's even cooler is that the local Māori community will manage these reserves, ensuring their traditional knowledge is respected. This initiative is a fantastic opportunity to boost marine biodiversity and create safe havens for wildlife.
