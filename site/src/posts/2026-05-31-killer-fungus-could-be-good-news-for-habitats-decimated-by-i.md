So, there's this exciting discovery of a 'killer fungus' in Britain that targets the invasive heath-star moss! This moss has been wreaking havoc on native habitats, but this new fungus could help restore balance. Dr. George Greiff, who found it, describes how it could be a turning point in the fight against invasive species. It’s amazing to think that something we often view as harmful could actually be a hero in this story!
