A physicist at the University of Birmingham has crafted a mini universe that challenges our understanding of time. In a groundbreaking study, Professor Giovanni Barontini shows that time can emerge from the behavior of a quantum system, rather than relying on a traditional clock. This experiment, using ultracold atoms, reveals that changes in entropy can define the flow of time. It's a fascinating leap forward in exploring the nature of time in quantum gravity!
