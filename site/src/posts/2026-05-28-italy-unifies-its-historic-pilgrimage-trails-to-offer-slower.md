Italy is taking a refreshing approach to tourism by uniting its historic pilgrimage trails into a network called Antichi Cammini d’Italia. This initiative promotes slower, more authentic travel experiences, allowing visitors to explore lesser-known areas while easing the pressure on popular tourist spots. With integrated smart signage and a digital hub, travelers can access information and maps in real time, enhancing their journey. It’s a fantastic way to connect with Italy's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.
