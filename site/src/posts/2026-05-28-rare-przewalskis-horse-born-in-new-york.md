On April 21, a delightful Przewalski’s horse foal was born at the Bronx Zoo in New York City, marking a significant moment for this endangered species. These unique horses, often mistaken for mules, have a fascinating history of survival, having once been declared extinct in the wild. Thanks to conservation efforts, including breeding programs, their population is slowly recovering, with this new foal adding to the hope for their future. Visitors can now catch a glimpse of this adorable addition from the Wild Asia Monorail, where it's sure to charm everyone who sees it.
