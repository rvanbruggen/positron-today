The restoration of the stunning Palazzo Farnese in Rome has finally been completed, and it looks absolutely magnificent! It's fascinating to think that this architectural gem, which has been around for 473 years, has been revived with the help of €8.5 million from the French government. Daniela Porro and the French Ambassador Anne-Marie Descotes celebrated this achievement, highlighting the collaborative spirit between France and Italy. Now, the palace shines brightly, free from scaffolding, symbolizing the strong Franco-Italian relationship.
