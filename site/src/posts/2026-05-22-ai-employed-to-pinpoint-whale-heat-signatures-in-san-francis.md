Scientists in California are using heat-sensing AI to protect grey whales in San Francisco Bay. With increasing sightings of these magnificent creatures, conservationists are eager to prevent ship strikes, a leading cause of whale deaths. The AI identifies the whales' heat signatures and shares this information with passing ships. This innovative approach aims to significantly reduce whale mortality and is already showing promising results.
