Isn't it amazing how technology can solve real-world problems? Two IIT graduates, Johns and Kannappa, have created EyeROV, a startup that builds AI-powered underwater robots to inspect coral reefs and other underwater structures. This innovation not only helps in infrastructure inspections but also plays a crucial role in studying and protecting marine ecosystems. With rising sea temperatures and coral bleaching, their work is more important than ever!
