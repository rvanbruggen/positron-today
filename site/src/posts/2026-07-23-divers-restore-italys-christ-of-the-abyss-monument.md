So, divers from Italy's police, navy, and emergency services have come together to clean the iconic Christ of the Abyss statue off the coast of San Fruttuoso. This 2.5-meter statue, created in 1954, serves as a touching memorial for those who lost their lives at sea. It's made from melted-down metals, giving it a unique history and significance. The annual cleaning is crucial to preserve this beautiful underwater monument, ensuring it continues to inspire and remind us of its purpose.
