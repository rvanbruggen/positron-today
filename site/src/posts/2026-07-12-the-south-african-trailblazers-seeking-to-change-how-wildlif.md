Pragna Parsotam-Kok and Noel Kok are on a mission to transform wildlife documentaries in Africa. After realizing the lack of African filmmakers in this space, they founded the Nature Environment and Wildlife Conservation Trust (NEWF) to empower local talent. Their efforts have led to training hundreds of filmmakers and divers, creating authentic narratives that reflect the continent's rich biodiversity. The recent opening of their dive and filmmaking center, eKhaya, marks a significant milestone in their journey to foster a new generation of storytellers.
