This is exciting news for anyone concerned about cholesterol levels! The FDA has approved a new pill called enlicitide that can lower harmful LDL cholesterol levels to around 50, which is significantly lower than what statins can achieve. This once-a-day pill is not only effective but also more affordable than its injectable counterpart. With clinical trials showing promising results and comparable side effects to a placebo, this could be a game-changer in heart health.
