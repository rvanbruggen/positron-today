So, the African blue economy is really making waves, generating over $298 billion each year! Hersen Nyambe from the African Union shared that this sector not only supports more than 40 million jobs but is projected to grow even more by 2063. Now, while there are challenges like funding and implementation, the potential is huge, especially with Africa's rich marine ecosystems. It's all about turning strategies into action, and the upcoming conference is a great step in that direction!
