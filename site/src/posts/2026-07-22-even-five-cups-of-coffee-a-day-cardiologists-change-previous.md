Cardiologists are revising their guidelines, suggesting that moderate coffee consumption could actually be beneficial for heart health. This is exciting news for coffee lovers, as it opens up new possibilities for enjoying our favorite brew. Which brings me to the idea that sometimes, what we believe about food and drink can change dramatically with new research.
