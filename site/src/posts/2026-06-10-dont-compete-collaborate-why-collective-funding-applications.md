There's a fascinating shift happening in the world of research funding. With more scientists applying for grants than ever, the competition is fierce, and many are left frustrated by low success rates. This article highlights the power of collaboration, suggesting that researchers should team up—even with their rivals—to tackle funding applications together. By working collectively, they can not only improve their chances of securing funds but also enhance the quality and impact of their research.
