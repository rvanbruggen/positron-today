In Uganda's Masaka region, coffee growers are experiencing a remarkable turnaround thanks to regenerative agriculture techniques. By implementing practices like mulching and cover cropping, farmers are not only battling drought but also enhancing the quality and yield of their coffee. This initiative, supported by a coalition including Nespresso, has established model farms that provide hands-on training for local growers. The results are already evident, with farmers reporting healthier trees and improved income stability.
