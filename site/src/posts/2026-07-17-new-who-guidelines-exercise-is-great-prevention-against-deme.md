So, the World Health Organization has released new guidelines emphasizing the importance of exercise as a key preventive measure against dementia. Isn't that exciting? It seems that staying active can really make a difference in our brain health! However, the guidelines also suggest that hormone therapy and vitamin supplements may not be as beneficial as previously thought. Which brings me to the idea that we should all get moving and prioritize our well-being!
