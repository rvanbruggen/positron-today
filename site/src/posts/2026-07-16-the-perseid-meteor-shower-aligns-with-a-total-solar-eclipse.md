Skygazers are in for an incredible experience this August as the renowned Perseid meteor shower coincides with a total solar eclipse! This year, the peak days for the meteor shower fall on August 12 and 13, which is just perfect because that’s when the eclipse happens too. Imagine witnessing bright meteors streaking across the sky while the moon casts its shadow over the sun—how magical is that? If you're in the path of totality, you'll get to enjoy this cosmic show for a couple of minutes, and even if you're not, the meteors will still be visible throughout the month.
