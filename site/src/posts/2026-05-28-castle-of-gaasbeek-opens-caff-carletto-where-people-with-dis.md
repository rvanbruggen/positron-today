So, there's a fantastic new café called Caffè Carletto at the Castle of Gaasbeek! It's a special place where people with disabilities can gain valuable work experience in the hospitality sector. The café is a collaboration between Zonnelied, Pajot Begot, and the castle itself, creating a warm and welcoming environment for everyone. It's all about helping these individuals connect more with society and learn new skills at their own pace.
