So, here's some fantastic news! The United States has just delivered lenacapavir, a groundbreaking new HIV prevention medication, to Malawi. This is a huge step in the fight against HIV/AIDS, with Malawi being one of only ten countries selected for this innovative program. Chargé d'Affaires Jonathan Fischer emphasized the importance of this delivery in achieving the goal of ending HIV/AIDS as a public health threat by 2030. It's truly inspiring to see such commitment to improving health systems and promoting self-reliance!
