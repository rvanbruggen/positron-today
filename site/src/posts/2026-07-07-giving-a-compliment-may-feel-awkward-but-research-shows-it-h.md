So, it turns out that giving compliments can be a win-win situation! Research shows that while many people hesitate to share kind words due to nerves, both the giver and receiver benefit from the experience. It’s fascinating how we often underestimate the positive impact our compliments can have on others. Plus, giving compliments can actually boost our own self-esteem and strengthen our connections with others.
