At the international press opening of Ruth Asawa's retrospective at the Guggenheim Bilbao, the excitement was palpable among attendees discovering her unique artistry. Asawa, a Japanese-American artist, masterfully wove wire into intricate sculptures, showcasing a blend of craftsmanship and creativity throughout her life. The exhibition, titled 'A lifetime for a workshop, a workshop for a lifetime,' highlights her innovative approach and the evolution of her work over the decades. From wire sculptures to clay and bronze molds, Asawa's artistic journey is a testament to her profound connection with nature and her relentless pursuit of originality.
