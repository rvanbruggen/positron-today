So, Gidi Littwin, who co-invented Apple's FaceID, is now on an exciting journey with his startup, Hemispheric. They've raised $52 million to develop an AI model that decodes brain activity, aiming to diagnose cognitive disorders without invasive procedures. By gathering data from 100,000 volunteers, they've created a model that can accurately assess brain health. This could revolutionize how we approach conditions like PTSD and Alzheimer's, making diagnosis as simple as a blood test!
