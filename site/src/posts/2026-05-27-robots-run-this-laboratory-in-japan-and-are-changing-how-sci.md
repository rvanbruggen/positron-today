In a groundbreaking lab in Tokyo, robots are taking on tasks like handling liquids and growing cells, thanks to their advanced AI. This innovative setup allows researchers to focus on more complex aspects of their work while the robots handle the repetitive stuff. The ultimate goal is to create a massive facility filled with thousands of these robots by 2040 or 2050, which is just thrilling! While humans still play a vital role in preparing materials and troubleshooting, the future of lab work looks bright with these intelligent machines.
