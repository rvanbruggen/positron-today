So, it turns out that belugas are joining the exclusive club of species that can recognize themselves in mirrors! This fascinating ability was observed in a recent study where researchers tested four belugas at a New York aquarium. Natasha and her daughter Maris showed curiosity and even social behaviors when they encountered their reflections. It's amazing to think that these intelligent creatures share this trait with humans and other social animals.
